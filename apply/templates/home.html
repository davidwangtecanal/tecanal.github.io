Join the TeCommunity

Apply to be a part of an organization run by students to benefit the community.


Executive Officer (EO)

You must be a rising sophomore, junior, or senior in high school to apply. All executive officers will be expected to volunteer at outreach sessions in Baltimore, MD regularly. Additionally, executive officers will have meetings at least twice a month. Some of these will be online, and some of these will be in-person in Howard County, MD.

Volunteer Coordinator (VoCo)

You must be a rising sophomore, junior, or senior in high school to apply. All volunteer coordinators will be expected to volunteer at outreach sessions in Baltimore, MD regularly. Additionally, volunteer coordinators will have meetings at least once a month. Some of these will be online, and some of these will be in-person in Howard County, MD.
