Frequently Asked Questions

Can I become a volunteer for TeCanal even though the school year has already started?

Of course you can! You can become a TeCanal volunteer at any point in the year. Just go to the Getting Started Guide to find information on how to join.

Do I get to choose when to come to outreach?

Yes, you only have to come to outreach when it is most convenient for you, no one will ever force you to go to outreach.
